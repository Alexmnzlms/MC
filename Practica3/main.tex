\subsection{Expresión Regular}
El sistema de ecuaciones\\
\begin{math}
x_{0} = 0x_{0} + 1x_{1}+\varepsilon \\
x_{1} = 1x_{0} + 0x_{2}\\
x_{2} = 0x_{1} + 1x_{2}\\
\\
x_{2}=1^{*}0x_{1}\\
\\
x_{1}=1x_{0}+0(1^{*}0x_{1})\\
x_{1}=1x_{0}+01^{*}0x_1{1}\\
x_{1}=(01^{*}0)^{*}1x_{0}\\
\\
x_{0}=0x_{0}+1(01^{*}0)^{*}1x_{0}+\varepsilon \\
x_{0}=x_{0}(0+1(01^{*}0)^{*}1)+\varepsilon\\
x_{0}=(0+1(01^{*}0)^{*}1)^{*}\\
\end{math}

La expresión regular resultante es \begin{math}
(0+1(01^{*}0)^{*}1)^{*}
\end{math}
